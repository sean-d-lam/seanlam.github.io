About Me

Hi! My name is Sean Dinh Lam, and I am a first-generation, low-income student looking to further my education and gain research experience. I am currently a freshman at Colorado College, where I intend on double majoring in Physics and Computer Science, with an emphasis in Astrophysics. Although, I am also considering studying Film & Media Studies. Aside from schooling, my daily activities include sports and work. I am a sprinter/hurdler/jumper on the Colorado College Men's Track & Field Team, and I am a Applied Science Research Intern for the United States Geological Survey (USGS) and Federal Geographic Data Committee (FGDC). My goal is to go into research/academia with a focus on astronomy or renewable energy.
